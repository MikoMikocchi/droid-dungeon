# Droid Dungeon

A cozy sci‑fi dungeon crawler about **us** and our loyal companion **Dotty**. We roam an endless dungeon, harvest resources, and turn them into a tiny industry while fending off hostile **enemies**.

## About

**Droid Dungeon** is a small, cute adventure built on **Java 21** + **LibGDX**. You explore a procedurally generated underground world, keep your gear ready, and let Dotty trail behind as your trusty buddy.

## Features

- Infinite, chunk‑based dungeon generation
- A sweet companion: **Dotty** follows your trail
- Inventory, pickup/drop, and equipment basics
- Melee combat against wandering enemies (Catsters)
- The core fantasy of building a tiny underground industry

## Run

- Desktop (LWJGL3): `./gradlew :desktop:run`
